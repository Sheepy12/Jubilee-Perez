What im all about!

Character design

Creating in depth visual illustrations of characters that serve a purpose in telling a conceptual story. Their design tells a story!

Digital Media

Creating a visual piece digitally has many advantages! for one, there is a large access of tools that are readily availiable at any time. using a tablet to draw can really bring a character out from the digital world and move into your own.

Comics

As an individual who loves to create stories, I find that a critical key component is making comics. This brings out your characters personalities and can show the audience the universe that they occupy.

Made with Love

All the pieces I create have a piece of me imputted within. Getting personal and getting the audience to feel something is my goal.
